# Multi-Channel Outbound Framework

## Why Multi-Channel?

Single-channel outreach fails due to:
- Inbox saturation
- Low attention
- Filtering

---

## Channels

1. Cold Email
2. LinkedIn
3. Cold Calls

---

## Example Sequence

Day 1 → Email  
Day 2 → LinkedIn connect  
Day 4 → Email follow-up  
Day 6 → Call  
Day 8 → LinkedIn message  

---

## LinkedIn Strategy

- Send personalized connection
- Engage with posts
- Send short message

---

## Video Prospecting (Optional)

- Short personalized videos
- Explain value quickly

---

## Key Principles

- Consistency across channels
- Same message, different format
- Don’t spam all at once

---

## Core Insight

> Multiple touchpoints increase familiarity and response rates.
