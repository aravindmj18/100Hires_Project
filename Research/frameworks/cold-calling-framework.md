# Cold Calling Framework

## Opening (First 10 seconds)

- Be direct
- Be human
- Remove pressure

Example:
“Hey [Name], this is Aravind — quick one, did I catch you at a bad time?”

---

## Pattern Break

Acknowledge interruption:

“I know I’m calling out of the blue…”

---

## Value Hook

Give reason to listen:

“We’re working with teams struggling to generate pipeline through outbound”

---

## Sell the “Blind Date”

Don’t pitch product — pitch insight:

“I’d love to connect you with someone who’s solved this”

---

## Discovery (If they engage)

Ask:
- How are you generating pipeline today?
- What’s working / not working?

---

## Objection Handling

Don’t push — defuse:

Example:
“Totally get that — most teams we speak to felt the same initially”

---

## Key Principles

- Tone > script
- Curiosity > pitching
- Conversation > conversion

---

## Core Insight

> Cold calls work when they feel like conversations, not sales pitches.
