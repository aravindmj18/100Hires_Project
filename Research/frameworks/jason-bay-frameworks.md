# Jason Bay — Cold Outreach Frameworks

## 1. First Impression Rule

- First 60–80 seconds decide success
- Bad opening = call fails immediately
- Avoid wasting time with useless questions

---

## 2. Avoid “Double/Triple Clutch”

❌ Bad:
- “Is this X?”
- “How are you?”
- “Do you have a minute?”

👉 Problem:
- No value
- Wastes first 30 seconds

---

## 3. Product Voice vs Customer Voice

❌ Product Voice:
- Talking about features
- Corporate language

✅ Customer Voice:
- Talk about their problems
- Use their language

---

## 4. Core Framework: Intro → Hook → Close

Cold call should follow:

1. Intro  
2. Hook  
3. Close  

---

## 5. Reason to Meet (RTM Framework)

### 3T Model:

- Trigger → Why now  
- Tension → Problem  
- Trust → Social proof  

---

## 6. Permission-Based Opener

Example:

- State reason  
- Ask for permission  

👉 Goal:
Get small “yes” (psychological buy-in)

---

## 7. Biggest Mistake

- Talking about yourself too early  
- “Prospecting narcissism”  

👉 Focus on:
- Prospect  
- Their situation
