# Cold Email Framework (Modern B2B SaaS)

## Structure

### 1. Hook (First Line)
- Personalized
- Relevant to prospect
- Shows research

Example:
“Noticed you’re hiring SDRs — usually means pipeline pressure”

---

### 2. Context / Problem
- Show understanding of their situation
- Highlight root cause

Example:
“Most teams struggle because outbound is inconsistent across reps”

---

### 3. Value / Insight
- Provide new perspective
- Show how others solved it

Example:
“We helped teams increase reply rates by fixing messaging, not volume”

---

### 4. Offer (Important)
Sell the **conversation**, not product

Example:
“Happy to share what’s working across similar teams”

---

### 5. CTA (Simple)
- Low friction
- No pressure

Example:
“Worth a quick chat next week?”

---

## Key Principles

- Keep it short (50–120 words)
- Avoid jargon
- One idea per email
- Write like a human, not marketing

---

## Personalization Types

1. Surface-level (name, company) ❌
2. Context-based (role, situation) ✅
3. Insight-based (problem + idea) 🔥

---

## Core Insight

> The goal of cold email is not to sell —  
> it’s to start a relevant conversation.
