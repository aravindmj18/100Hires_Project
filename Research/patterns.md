# Patterns

## Pattern 1: System > Activity

All experts emphasize that outbound success depends
on system design, not activity volume.

---

## Pattern 2: Personalization > Scale

High-performing outbound relies on relevance and
context, not mass messaging.

---

## Pattern 3: Execution Gaps > Strategy Gaps

Most companies know what to do,
but fail due to inconsistent execution.

---

## Pattern 4: Buyer Psychology is Critical

Understanding how buyers think is more important
than what tools or templates are used.

---

## Pattern 5: Multi-Channel Wins

Combining email, LinkedIn, and calls consistently
outperforms single-channel approaches.
