99% of Cold Emails suck. Only 1% get it right.

5 things I do to write top 1% emails:

1. <5th grade reading level
- If your prospect has to think what you mean, you've already lost them. 

2. A reason to reach out right now.
- Not "I help companies like yours". Reference a signal, post they wrote, etc.

3. 1 Social proof point
- Not three. Specific company. Specific result. Specific timeline. That's it.

4. 40 words
- Every sentence you add is a reason to stop reading.

5. 1 Yes/No CTA
No "let's jump on a call." No "book a 30-min demo." Just an open door.

Example:

Hi Helen, saw your post about DMs. You're getting engagement but I couldn't find how you turn it into actual clients. A coach I work went from chasing likes to booking 23 calls in a 11 days. Made you a 9 min video breaking down step-by-step how you can do it too - link. Worth exploring further? 

Oh, one more thing before you even hit send - figure out deliverability.
