# Observations

## 1. Outbound is Broken (But Not Dead)
Almost every expert agrees that traditional outbound
(volume-based) is no longer effective.

---

## 2. Messaging is the Biggest Gap
Most teams fail not due to lack of tools,
but due to weak positioning and unclear messaging.

---

## 3. Deliverability is Underrated
Technical setup is often ignored,
yet it directly impacts campaign success.

---

## 4. Leadership Involvement Matters
Outbound performance improves when leaders
actively participate and guide the process.

---

## 5. Multi-Channel is Becoming Standard
Email alone is no longer enough — LinkedIn and calls
are now essential.
